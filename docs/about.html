About Us

Lesya International LLC provides community-based services through Supported Community Connections, and Individual Residential Services & Supports.

Lesya International LLC will work with each person to design and lead their own person-centered plan to maximize independence and maintain health and safety. Services and supports will vary according to the person's own plan and support needs, but may include the following:
• Community Integration
• Skill Development
• Socialization
• Healthy Living
• Activities of Daily Living
• Education and Self-Advocacy
• Medication Administration
• Money Management Skills
• Behavioral Support
• Transportation

Who We Are

Lasya International LLC is a Residential Service Agency that supports adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities enrolled in Colorado. Our host home is located in the Denver-Metro Area.Our area of focus is providing appropriate residential settings and engaging community access opportunities for our clients. Our agency is focused on ensuring all individuals have access to a safe, supportive and flourishing living situation which encourages their independence and personal growth. i


We work with individuals enrolled in the following Community Centered Boards (CCBs) North Metro Community Services (NMCS) Developmental Pathways (DP) Rocky Mountain Human Services (RMHS)

Mission Statement To provide the highest level of individualized residential and community support to those with intellectual and developmental disabilities. We provide engaging and personalized programs that result in the highest level of social, physical, and emotional independence in one’s life


Individual Residential Services and support (IRSS) Lesya International LLC provides Residential support services through Host Homes and PCA (Personal Care Alternative) living environments. The Host Home program begins with a highly personalized matching process and focuses on the personal needs and choices of our clients. We assess our client’s interests, skills, and support needs to ensure the appropriate match that allows our clients to live life to the fullest in their community. Our goal is always to support in the development of a meaningful, long-term relationship between our clients and their Host Home Provider. That relationship should provide compassionate care, high quality of life, dedication, foster independence, and ensure the safety and wellbeing of those we support. We also offer Family Caregiver The Family Caregiver program model supports our client living with a family member who provides residential services to them in their family home. This option provides individuals the choice to continue living at home where they are comfortable and with familiar caregivers.


Supported Community Connections (SCC) supports the abilities and skills necessary for individuals to access typical activities and community life. We emphasize helping individuals to get involved in their community by connecting to resources and supporting them in their efforts.Ultimately it is a self-directed program and the people receiving services will determine how they choose to spend their time receiving their SCC services Our plan includes: Socialization Meet people who have similar interests and hobbies Find and maintain healthy friendships and natural supports Access community education or training Join and participate in community groups and committees Volunteer in your community
