About Us

Lesya International LLC provides community-based services through Supported Community Connections, and Individual Residential Services & Supports.

Lesya International LLC will work with each person to design and lead their own person-centered plan to maximize independence and maintain health and safety. Services and supports will vary according to the person's own plan and support needs, but may include the following:
• Community Integration
• Skill Development
• Socialization
• Healthy Living
• Activities of Daily Living
• Education and Self-Advocacy
• Medication Administration
• Money Management Skills
• Behavioral Support
• Transportation

Who We Are

Lasya International LLC is a Residential Service Agency that supports adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities enrolled in Colorado. Our host home is located in the Denver-Metro Area.Our area of focus is providing appropriate residential settings and engaging community access opportunities for our clients. Our agency is focused on ensuring all individuals have access to a safe, supportive and flourishing living situation which encourages their independence and personal growth. i


We work with individuals enrolled in the following Community Centered Boards (CCBs):
North Metro Community Services (NMCS)
Developmental Pathways (DP)
 Rocky Mountain Human Services (RMHS)


Mission Statement

To provide the highest level of individualized residential and community support to those with intellectual and developmental disabilities. We provide engaging and personalized programs that result in the highest level of social, physical, and emotional independence in one’s life.
